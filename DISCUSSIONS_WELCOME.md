# Welcome to SAB Audio Discussions

Welcome to the community hub for **SAB Audio**, an iCloud-native hi-fi audio player for iPhone and iPad.

## Use This Space For

- Setup questions and troubleshooting
- Feedback, bug reports, and feature requests
- Sharing smart-playlist rule recipes and EQ presets
- Audiophile tips — DAC pairings, AutoEQ profiles, room observations
- Audiobook recommendations and listening flow ideas

## Before You Post

Please remove anything you do not want public from screenshots or attachments:

- Personal email addresses or full names
- Track titles you would rather not share
- Anything else specific to your environment

For private support, email **[support@youritsolution.dk](mailto:support@youritsolution.dk)** directly instead of opening a public discussion.

## Bug Report Template

When reporting a bug, the most useful template is:

> **What I did:** I tapped X then Y
> **What I expected:** Z should have happened
> **What actually happened:** A
> **Device:** iPhone 15 Pro, iOS 17.4
> **Library size (approximate):** 4,000 tracks
> **Reproduces every time / sometimes:** every time

That's enough. We do not need diagnostic dumps or device logs in most cases.

Thanks for being part of the SAB Audio community.
